The Hemingway Daiquiri

Description

I'm a bartender full time while I learn to code, and this is one of my favorite cocktails to make

It's named after the famouos author, and tastes sweet yet dry, tangy yet refreshing. This is something that could be enjoyed all year round

Ingredients

  • A coupe or martini glass
  • Cocktail tins and cocktail strainer
  • 2 oz white rum
  • 3/4 oz lime juice
  • 1/2 oz maraschino liqueur
  • 1/2 oz grapefruit juice
  • Lime wheel for garnish

Steps

  1. To your cocktail tins add ice and all of the ingredients except the lime wheel, and shake until well chilled
  2. Strain into your coupe or martini glass
  3. Garnish with the lime wheel
