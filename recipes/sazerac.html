The Sazerac

Description

The sazerac is neck and neck with the hemingway as one of my favorite cocktails

I love how boozy it is and it offers many different flavor profiles if you change the base spirit to a cognac, or even a bourbon

Ingredients

  • A rocks glass
  • A mixing glass (or a pint glass will do)
  • A bar spoon (can use anything to mix the cocktail at home)
  • A cocktail strainer
  • Absinthe to rinse
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • 4 dashes peychaud's bitters
  • 2 oz rye whiskey
  • Lemon peel for a garnish

Steps

  1. Rinse a chilled rocks glass with absinthe, discarding the excess and setting the glass aside
  2. In your mixing glass, add the whiskey, simple syrup, peychaud's bitters, and ice
  3. Stir until well chilled
  4. Strain the cocktail into the prepared rocks glass
  5. Express the lemon peel over the cocktail, rub it around the rim, drop it in the drink, and enjoy!
