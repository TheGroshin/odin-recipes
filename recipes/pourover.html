Pour Over Coffee

Description

I really love coffee, all types. My favorite of all is a very classic pour over.

In the following sections i'll explain ingredients and my way of making pour over coffee, to hopefully inspire others to try something new

Ingredients

  • 30g Ground coffee beans, either freshly ground or pre-ground (medium-coarse)
  • 578g Filtered water heated to 200 degrees Fahrenheit (93.333 Celcius)
  • Pinch of salt
  • A pour over device (having a permanent filter or paper filter does little to change the taste, so personal preference on that purchase)

Steps

  1. Heat water to 200 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Pre-heat pour over container with some of the heated water and empty
  3. Place filter onto pour over device, and add the coffee grounds and salt to the filter
  4. In an even swirling motion pour some water over the grounds (just enough to ensure all grounds are wet), and wait 30 secs for coffee grounds to bloom
  5. After the 30 secs begin pouring water over coffee grounds in an even swirling motion again, taking breaks to not pour too much and over/under extract coffee
  6. After all of the water is through the grounds, take the filter container off the pour over device and enjoy your freshly brewed coffee!
